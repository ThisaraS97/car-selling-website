How to Use FormSubmit.co with Your Website

Step 1: Update Your Form Action

Modify your contact form to use FormSubmit by changing the action attribute:

<form method="POST" action="https://formsubmit.co/your-email@example.com">
    <!-- Your existing form fields -->
</form>

Replace your-email@example.com with the email address where you want to receive submissions.

Step 2: Activate Your FormSubmit Account

The first time someone submits the form, you'll receive an activation email from FormSubmit. Click the activation link to confirm your email address.

Step 3: Add Optional Features

FormSubmit offers several optional features using hidden fields:

Custom Subject Line:

<input type="hidden" name="_subject" value="New inquiry from ABC Motors website">

Custom Success Page:

<input type="hidden" name="_next" value="https://your-website.com/success.html">

Enable CAPTCHA:

<input type="hidden" name="_captcha" value="true">

Custom Email Template:

<input type="hidden" name="_template" value="box">

Honeypot to Prevent Spam:

<input type="text" name="_honey" style="display:none">

Complete Form Example

<form method="POST" action="https://formsubmit.co/your-email@example.com">
    <input type="hidden" name="_subject" value="New inquiry from ABC Motors website">
    <input type="hidden" name="_next" value="https://your-website.com/success.html">
    <input type="hidden" name="_captcha" value="true">
    <input type="hidden" name="_template" value="box">
    <input type="text" name="_honey" style="display:none">
    
    <div>
        <label for="name">Name</label>
        <input type="text" name="name" id="name" required>
    </div>
    
    <div>
        <label for="email">Email</label>
        <input type="email" name="email" id="email" required>
    </div>
    
    <div>
        <label for="subject">Subject</label>
        <input type="text" name="subject" id="subject" required>
    </div>
    
    <div>
        <label for="message">Message</label>
        <textarea name="message" id="message" rows="4" required></textarea>
    </div>
    
    <button type="submit">Send Message</button>
</form>

Advantages of FormSubmit

  • Free: No registration required, unlimited submissions
  • Simple Setup: Just change the form action
  • Anti-spam: Built-in spam protection
  • File Uploads: Supports file attachments
  • Custom Redirects: Redirect to a thank you page
  • No Backend Code: Works with static sites

Limitations

  • Your email address is visible in the HTML source (though they offer an alias feature for the paid plan)
  • Limited customization of email templates in the free tier
  • Cannot store submissions (emails only)
