FormSubmit Implementation

To implement FormSubmit on your car-selling website, follow these steps:

  1. Replace the existing contact form in your index.html file with the code below
  2. Replace YOUR_EMAIL@EXAMPLE.COM with your actual email address
  3. Update https://your-site-url.netlify.app/success.html with your actual website URL

How FormSubmit Works

FormSubmit is a free service that handles form submissions and forwards them to your email. When someone submits your contact form:

  1. The form data is sent to FormSubmit's servers
  2. FormSubmit processes the data and sends you an email with the details
  3. The user is redirected to your success page

First Submission

The first time someone submits the form, you'll receive an activation email from FormSubmit. You must click the activation link to start receiving form submissions. This prevents spam and unauthorized use.

After implementing FormSubmit, make a test submission yourself to receive and activate the confirmation email.

For more information, visit FormSubmit.co.
