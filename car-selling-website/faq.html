FAQ

What documents do I need to rent a car?

To rent a car, you'll need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name, and a form of identification (passport or government-issued ID). International travelers may need an International Driving Permit along with their home country license. All drivers must be at least 21 years old, though additional fees may apply for drivers under 25.

What is included in the rental price?

Our rental prices include basic insurance coverage, unlimited mileage within the rental area, 24/7 roadside assistance, and standard vehicle maintenance. Additional services like GPS navigation, child seats, additional drivers, and premium insurance can be added for an extra fee. Fuel is not included - you'll need to return the vehicle with the same fuel level.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your pickup time without any penalty. Changes made within 24 hours or no-shows may incur additional charges. We recommend making changes as early as possible to ensure vehicle availability for your new dates and times.

What happens if I return the car late?

Late returns are subject to additional charges. We offer a 30-minute grace period for returns, after which late fees apply. If you're more than 2 hours late, you'll be charged for an additional day. Please contact us as soon as possible if you expect to return the vehicle late to discuss options and avoid extra charges.

Do you offer one-way rentals?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals to many locations within our service area. One-way rentals may include an additional drop-off fee depending on the pickup and return locations. When booking, simply select different pickup and drop-off locations to see availability and any applicable fees for your desired route.

What should I do in case of an accident or breakdown?

In case of an accident, ensure everyone's safety first, then contact local emergency services if needed. Contact our 24/7 customer service immediately to report the incident. For breakdowns, call our roadside assistance number provided with your rental agreement. We'll arrange for repairs or a replacement vehicle as quickly as possible. Never leave the scene without proper documentation.
