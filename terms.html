Please read our terms carefully before using our services.

1. Rental Agreement and Eligibility

By renting a vehicle from our company, you agree to be bound by these terms and conditions. All renters must be at least 21 years of age and possess a valid driver's license for a minimum of 2 years. Drivers under 25 may be subject to additional fees. A valid credit card in the renter's name is required for all transactions. International renters must provide a valid International Driving Permit along with their home country license.

2. Vehicle Use and Restrictions

Rental vehicles may only be operated by authorized drivers listed on the rental agreement. The vehicle must not be used for commercial purposes, racing, off-road driving, or any illegal activities. Smoking is strictly prohibited in all vehicles. The vehicle must not be taken outside the designated rental area without prior written approval. Violation of these restrictions may result in immediate termination of the rental agreement and additional charges.

3. Insurance and Liability Coverage

All rentals include basic insurance coverage as required by law. Additional coverage options are available for purchase. The renter is responsible for any damage to the vehicle during the rental period, including theft, vandalism, or accidents. A security deposit will be held on your credit card and released upon satisfactory return of the vehicle. You are liable for all traffic violations, parking tickets, and tolls incurred during the rental period.

4. Payment Terms and Cancellation Policy

Payment is due at the time of rental and will be charged to the credit card on file. Reservations can be cancelled without penalty up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time. Cancellations within 24 hours or no-shows will be charged a cancellation fee equal to one day's rental. Late returns will incur additional daily charges. All prices are subject to applicable taxes and fees.

5. Vehicle Return and Fuel Policy

Vehicles must be returned on or before the agreed return date and time to avoid additional charges. The vehicle should be returned with the same fuel level as at pickup; otherwise, refueling charges will apply at premium rates. The vehicle must be returned in the same condition as received, allowing for normal wear and tear. Any damage, excessive cleaning requirements, or missing items will result in additional charges to your credit card.
